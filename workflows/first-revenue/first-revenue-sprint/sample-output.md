# Sample Outcome: First Revenue Sprint

## 1. Service Offer
**AI Automation Auditing for Small B2B Agencies**
- Offer: Audit current sales and client ingestion workflow to locate 3 areas where they are losing leads, and build a simple n8n automation framework to capture them.
- Pricing: 50,000 KZT per audit (Goal: 2 sales to hit 100,000 KZT).

## 2. Target Client Profile
- Owners of small local marketing agencies, translation bureaus, or web design studios with 3-10 team members.

## 3. 7-Day Sprint Plan
- **Day 1**: Assemble list of 20 warm B2B contacts and agency owners from Telegram channels and LinkedIn.
- **Day 2**: Audit your own workflow tools; prepare a 1-page "Common Lead Leak Points" checklist PDF.
- **Day 3**: Send personalized outreach to first 10 contacts offering a free 15-minute lead-leak diagnostic.
- **Day 4**: Follow up with the first 10 contacts; send outreach to next 10 contacts.
- **Day 5**: Run diagnostic calls; present custom 50,000 KZT implementation roadmap.
- **Day 6**: Close deals; collect 50% upfront payment.
- **Day 7**: Deliver first audit and automation plan; request referrals.

## 4. Outreach Template (Telegram/LinkedIn)
> "Hi [Name], noticed your agency is scaling up projects on LinkedIn. I'm running a short sprint this week helping 3 agencies run a quick 'lead leak check' on their current contact forms. It takes 15 mins, no sales pitch, and you get a 1-page breakdown of any missed follow-up gaps. Let me know if you'd be open to checking it out tomorrow?"
