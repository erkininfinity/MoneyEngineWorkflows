# Sample Outcome: Lost Deal Analyzer

## Diagnostic Report

### Top 3 Loss Reasons
1. **Pricing too high vs. competition** (33% of deals) — Delta Systems mentioned competitor pricing directly.
2. **No urgency / DIY decision** (33% of deals) — Common with mid-market clients who feel capable in-house.
3. **Lost to larger agencies** (33% of deals) — Perception gap around capacity and credibility.

### Offer Weaknesses
- Proposals lack social proof (no case studies cited).
- No risk reversal mechanism — clients fear committing to an unknown vendor.
- Value-to-cost ratio isn't justified clearly in cold outreach.

### Pricing Analysis
- Average lost deal value: **376,666 KZT** — suggests positioning may be aimed at mid-market too early.
- Cold outreach deals tend to be larger but close at 0% — warm leads perform better.

### Lead Source Patterns
- LinkedIn cold outreach: 2 lost deals, high-value, 0% close rate.
- Referral: 1 lost deal, medium value, lost due to no urgency.

### 3 Experiments for Next Sprint
1. Add 2 client case studies to all proposals before sending.
2. Introduce a "Pilot Project" tier at 90,000 KZT to reduce commitment friction.
3. Shift cold outreach budget to follow-up on warm/referral leads first.
